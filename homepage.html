About

Tinkerhub Foundation is a non profit organisation aimed at making use of 21st century technologies and learning methods to foster a fresh breed of highly skilled young people empowered with technical and social skills.
WIT - Women In Tech ,a part of Tinkerhub , aims on giving technical and non-technical assistance of various fields for girls. We help students learn as they advance and realise their own ideas and projects.



Who can join?

Join requirement

* Any College or School or Pass-out students
* Only for girls
* Must be a member of TinkerHub
* Must join at TinkerHub discord and verify your membership id
